OUR MISSION

GLOBAL COMPUTER INSTITUTE is a premier Computer Institute in Karachi. Our aim is to empower the youth with the powerful weapon that is computer science by offering some exceptionally good computer courses that will enhance their skill set and lead to terrific job opportunities. By enhancing your skill set in the rapidly growing field of Information technology, you are benefited in more than one ways, you can simply learn a new skill to add to your existing qualifications or you can opt for a course that would lead to a job on its own.

Our vision and mission is to empower PAKISTAN through technology. I.T. when rightly employed leads to productivity improvements and prosperity at individual, organizational, societal, National and Global level. GCI has embarked on a business excellence initiative and is investing in developing competencies to deliver superior quality with total customer orientation. Our Customer Policy is a simple one, Anticipate and exceed customer expectations through effective and timely delivery of industry relevant products and services.
